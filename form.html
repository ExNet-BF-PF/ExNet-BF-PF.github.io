ExNet-BF+PF: Explainable DNN-based Beamformer with Postfilter



This web page is intended to support a subjective evaluation of audio quality using the Mean Opinion Score (MOS) methodology. Listeners are invited to assess and compare audio samples processed by our proposed method (ExNet-BF+PF) with two existing baseline approaches: FasNet and SpatialNet.



To reduce potential bias, users are given the choice of three different evaluation pages. Each page contains a unique set of audio samples, and the order of methods is randomized across pages. For each audio clip, listeners are asked to rate the perceived quality on a scale from 1 (Poor) to 5 (Excellent).

The collected ratings will be used to analyze and compare the effectiveness of each method in enhancing speech signals under various conditions.
