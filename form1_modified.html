ExNet-BF+PF: Explainable DNN-based Beamformer with Postfilter



Form Page

This web page is intended to support a subjective evaluation of audio quality using the Mean Opinion Score (MOS) methodology. Listeners are invited to assess and compare audio samples processed by our proposed method (ExNet-BF+PF) with two existing baseline approaches: FasNet and SpatialNet.

For each audio clip, listeners are asked to rate the perceived quality on a scale from 1 (Poor) to 5 (Excellent).
